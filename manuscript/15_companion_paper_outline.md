# Manuscript Outline for the AII Companion Paper

A proposed paper outline targeting *Entropy*, *Neural Computation*, or *Frontiers in Computational Neuroscience*, designed for ~25–35 pages of main text plus appendices.

**Title.** *Policy Entanglement in Active Inference: A Tunable Mean-Field Deformation Framework with Information-Geometric and Spectral Structure*

**1. Introduction.** Multi-stream policies, the embodiment-vs-parametric-coupling choice, framing.

**2. Mean-field policy ensembles.** Setup, single-stream review, MF baseline.

**3. The $\lambda$-deformation.** Definition, boundary cases, role of coupling potentials.

**4. The entanglement decomposition.** [[THMREF:thm_4_1]] with proof; corollaries.

**5. Information geometry.** e/m-flatness; e-geodesic family; revertibility.

**6. Spectral structure.** Schmidt rank, archetypal modes, tensor decompositions for $K > 2$.

**7. Heterogeneous inference.** VFE/EFE mixed ensembles; [[THMREF:thm_8_1]] with proof.

**8. Three-level update hierarchy.** Within-stream, coupling, precision.

**9. Phase structure and clinical predictions.** Order parameters, regimes, dysregulation.

**10. Connections to existing frameworks.** Hierarchical AIF, sophisticated inference, BTAI, KL control, options, PoE, copula VI.

**11. Empirical case studies.** $K=2$ closed form; motor+attention; multi-timescale; heterogeneous; habit learning.

**12. Lean formalization.** Phased plan; [[THMREF:thm_4_1]] as first machine-checked milestone.

**13. Open problems.**

**14. Discussion.** The parametric-entanglement worldview; alignment implications; biological plausibility.

**Appendices.**
- A: Full proof of [[THMREF:thm_4_1]].
- B: Convexity conditions ([[THMREF:thm_4_3]]).
- C: $K=2$ Bernoulli closed form.
- D: Tensor-train inference algorithms.
- E: Lean code listings.
